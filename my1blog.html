1- How to make Responsive Navbar in 2mins ?

you can use bootstrap to make responsive navbar in 2 mins it is an online css framework which helps us to make website very quickly.


Step 1 :Open your browser and search getbootstrap.com click here to visit bootstrap
Step 2 : Then copy the starter template by clicking on the copy option and paste it in your favourite IDE I support Vs code .





Step 3 : After pasting it in IDE then again go to bootstrap and choose components option then In components click on navbar .





Step 4 : Click on copy option an passte the navbar in the IDE and your Responsive Navbar is ready.
